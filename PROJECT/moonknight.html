Storyline

"Moon Knight" follows **Steven Grant**, a mild-mannered museum employee who suffers from blackouts and memories of another life. Soon, he discovers that he has a dissociative identity disorder and shares a body with **Marc Spector**, a mercenary who is the earthly avatar of the Egyptian moon god **Khonshu**. As Steven and Marc struggle for control of their shared body, they find themselves caught in a deadly battle involving Egyptian gods, ancient mysteries, and a sinister villain, **Arthur Harrow**. The show explores themes of identity, trauma, and mental health as Steven/Marc navigates the complex world of gods, cults, and personal discovery. With action-packed sequences and a deep dive into the psyche of the protagonist, "Moon Knight" takes viewers on a journey through the psychological chaos of its central character while introducing new elements of the MCU.
