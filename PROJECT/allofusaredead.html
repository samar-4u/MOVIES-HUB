Storyline

"All of Us Are Dead" is a South Korean zombie apocalypse drama that follows a group of high school students who find themselves trapped inside their school when a zombie virus outbreak suddenly occurs. The virus begins in a high school laboratory as an experimental biological agent but quickly spreads to the students and faculty. As the infection spreads rapidly throughout the school, the survivors must work together to escape while fighting for their lives. The show follows **Cheong San** and his friends, including **Nam Ra**, **On Jo**, and **Su Hyeok**, as they navigate the chaos, fend off hordes of zombies, and try to figure out how to survive while facing emotional and physical challenges. Meanwhile, outside the school, the military is trying to contain the outbreak, and the students must contend with the rapidly deteriorating situation inside. The series explores themes of survival, friendship, and the psychological toll of living in a world overrun by the undead.
