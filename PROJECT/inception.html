Storyline

Dom Cobb is a skilled thief, the best in the dangerous art of "extraction," a process where secrets are stolen from within a person's subconscious while they dream. His abilities have made him a coveted player in industrial espionage, but they have also cost him everything he loves. Cobb is offered a chance to have his past crimes erased if he can successfully perform "inception"—the planting of an idea in someone’s mind, a feat considered to be impossible. Cobb assembles a team to carry out the task, which involves navigating multiple layers of dreams within dreams. As the mission progresses, the line between dream and reality begins to blur, and Cobb must confront his own deepest fears in order to succeed. The film explores themes of memory, guilt, and the nature of reality.
