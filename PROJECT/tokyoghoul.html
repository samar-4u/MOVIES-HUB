Storyline

Tokyo Ghoul follows Ken Kaneki, a shy, bookish college student, who survives a near-fatal encounter with Rize Kamishiro, a ghoul. Ghouls are creatures that resemble humans but must consume human flesh to survive. After the incident, Ken is turned into a half-ghoul, forced to live in the shadows of both the human and ghoul worlds. As he struggles to come to terms with his new identity, Ken is pulled into the dangerous, complex world of ghouls, where he must fight to survive while also trying to retain his humanity. The series explores themes of identity, the nature of good and evil, and the conflict between the human and ghoul races.
