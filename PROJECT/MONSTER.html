Storyline

Dr. Kenzo Tenma, a brilliant neurosurgeon, saves the life of a young boy named Johan Liebert, who has been shot in the head. However, this decision leads to a series of tragic events. Johan grows up to become a ruthless and charismatic psychopath, wreaking havoc across Europe. As Dr. Tenma embarks on a journey to stop Johan and confront his own conscience, he becomes embroiled in a dangerous cat-and-mouse game with a man whose intelligence and manipulation skills far surpass his own. The anime is a psychological thriller that explores themes of morality, guilt, and the nature of evil.
