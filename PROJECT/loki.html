Storyline

"Loki" is a Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) television series that follows the God of Mischief, **Loki**, after his escape with the Tesseract in *Avengers: Endgame* (2019). This series takes place outside the main MCU timeline, as it follows Loki on his journey through alternate timelines and dimensions. After stealing the Tesseract, Loki is apprehended by the **Time Variance Authority (TVA)**, an organization that manages the multiverse and ensures that timelines remain intact. The TVA informs Loki that his escape has caused a "variant" timeline that threatens to unravel the fabric of time itself. Throughout the series, Loki is forced to team up with TVA agent **Mobius M. Mobius** (played by **Owen Wilson**) to help track down a mysterious variant of himself, who is causing chaos across the multiverse. As Loki unravels the secrets of the TVA, he is confronted with questions about his identity, purpose, and his future in the MCU. The series explores themes of self-discovery, destiny, and the consequences of tampering with time.
