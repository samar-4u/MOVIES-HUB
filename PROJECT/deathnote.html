Storyline

"Death Note" follows Light Yagami, a brilliant but disillusioned high school student who discovers a mysterious notebook that allows its user to kill anyone whose name is written in it. With this power, Light embarks on a self-proclaimed mission to rid the world of criminals and create a utopia where he reigns as a god. However, his actions attract the attention of an eccentric and highly intelligent detective known as L, leading to a deadly cat-and-mouse game. Themes of justice, morality, and the nature of power are explored throughout the series.
