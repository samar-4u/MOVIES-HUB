Storyline

"3 Idiots" is a heartwarming comedy-drama that follows the lives of three engineering students—**Rancho**, **Farhan**, and **Raju**—at a prestigious college. The film primarily focuses on the unconventional approach to education and the intense pressure faced by students in the Indian educational system. Rancho, a free-spirited and curious student, constantly challenges traditional academic methods, advocating for learning through creativity, passion, and understanding rather than rote memorization. The film alternates between flashbacks of their college days and the present, where Farhan and Raju reunite with Rancho years after graduation. Through their journey, they confront personal and societal expectations, societal pressures, and the concept of success, all while uncovering the true meaning of life, happiness, and following one's dreams. "3 Idiots" is a poignant reminder of the importance of pursuing one's passion over conformity.
