Storyline

"Jujutsu Kaisen" is a supernatural action anime set in a world where cursed spirits prey on humanity. The story follows **Yuji Itadori**, a kind-hearted high school student who gains extraordinary physical abilities. However, when his friends unwittingly unseal a cursed object, Yuji becomes involved in a dangerous battle against malevolent spirits. To save his friends, Yuji swallows a cursed object — the finger of a powerful curse known as **Ryomen Sukuna**, who then becomes partially bound to him. After this fateful act, Yuji is taken under the wing of **Satoru Gojo**, a teacher at the **Jujutsu High School**, where students are trained to fight curses. With the help of his friends, Yuji must navigate the dangerous world of curses, while learning about the dark history and the rules governing these malevolent forces. The series combines intense combat, rich world-building, and compelling character development.
