Storyline

"Happy New Year" is a Bollywood action-comedy film that follows the story of a group of misfits who team up to pull off a heist. The film's protagonist, **Charlie**, is a man with a personal vendetta against a business tycoon who has wronged him and his family. Charlie assembles a team of unlikely criminals, including **Shah Rukh Khan**, **Deepika Padukone**, **Abhishek Bachchan**, **Boman Irani**, **Sonu Sood**, and **Vivaan Shah**, all of whom have unique skills that contribute to the grand heist they plan to execute. The group enters a prestigious diamond heist competition in Dubai, and through a series of comic and action-packed sequences, they must outwit their enemies and succeed in the mission. As they embark on this mission, they also deal with personal motivations, camaraderie, and love.
